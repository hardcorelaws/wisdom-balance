# Control of the Official Framework

*The Wisdom Balance* is an authored framework.

The official version is maintained by Puneet Kuthiala. 

Contributions,suggestions, examples, and improvements are welcome, but changes to the official framework are accepted only at the author’s discretion.

The author retains final decision rights over the official wording, diagrams, structure, templates, version history, and future releases of The Wisdom Balance.

Adaptations are permitted under the license, but adaptations do not become part of the official framework unless accepted into the official repository.
