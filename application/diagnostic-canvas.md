## Assessment Questions

1. **Visible pressure:** What pressure is appearing?

   

2. **People:** What human condition is influencing the pressure?

   

3. **Decisions:** What decision is delayed, weak, unclear, or creating consequences?

   

4. **Shared Values:** What value, conduct, trust, or cultural issue is being tested?

   

5. **Controls:** What governance, reporting, escalation, or control issue is helping or hurting?

   

6. **Imbalance:** Which force is weak, excessive, neglected, or in conflict?

   

7. **Wise response:** What action would restore balance without creating more serious damage?

   

8. **Reassessment:** When should this be reviewed again?
