# The Wisdom Balance Quick Start

1. What visible pressure is appearing?
2. What deeper force may be causing it?
3. Are people aligned, tired, cautious, resistant, or compensating?
4. What decision has been delayed, avoided, or poorly understood?
5. What value is being tested under pressure?
6. Are controls revealing reality or preserving appearances?
7. What wise action would restore balance?



## Example scenarios where this model can be applied:



### Example 1: Schedule delay

**Visible pressure:** delay.
**Deeper imbalance:** decisions unclear, stakeholders misaligned.
**Rational response:** clarify decision rights, reset expectations, improve escalation.

### Example 2: Cost pressure

**Visible pressure:** rising cost.
**Deeper imbalance:** scope ownership unclear, controls weak.
**Rational response:** confirm ownership, strengthen change control, revisit assumptions.

### Example 3: Governance theatre

**Visible pressure:** many reports, little movement.
**Deeper imbalance:** controls excessive but insight weak.
**Rational response:** reduce ritual reporting, strengthen decision-focused governance.
